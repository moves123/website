How secure is the site?

The website in question is very secure and takes the security of their customers/members as a priority. Since they have joined the internet business model they haven’t had any problems with cyber criminals targeting them and leaking user’s information. As the internet is growing they are also keeping up with the new improved features to protect their users as well for instance they use SSL Certificate on their site and by doing this its makes it more secure for customers because it allows a secure connection between the user browser and web server. With that it makes it secure for customers to login to the site and buy things online using their cards knowing their details are protected.

Also with SSL certificate it makes it easier to distinguish a real website from a fake one which is vital for their customers because there are lots of phishing attempts online. They are also very strict on their password use on the site. They make all their users to create a very long and obscure password that would hard to guess. Every password on the site should be over 8 characters long, have special symbols in them like for example “@£$%&”, A capital letter character and finally a digit in there which makes the password more secure and more or less difficult to crack or guess.
